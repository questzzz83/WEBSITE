Gesture-Controlled Interface

Hand tracking running entirely in your browser via MediaPipe. Your camera feed is processed on-device — nothing is uploaded or stored anywhere.

Click Start camera and allow access. Hold one hand up so it's visible in the small camera box. Move your index fingertip to steer the dot, and pinch your thumb and index finger together while over a tile to click it.
