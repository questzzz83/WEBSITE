HOW TO PLAY

  • Find 3 gas cans to fuel the escape car
  • Stay on roads when possible - movement is faster
  • Avoid zombies - they will damage you on contact
  • Buildings are obstacles - navigate around them
  • Find the escape car (red sports car) once you have gas
  • Use Q to shoot zombies and stun them temporarily
  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move
  • Press SPACE to interact with objects
