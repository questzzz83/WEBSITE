How to Use This Scanner

  1. Click "Start Camera" to enable your device's camera (requires permission)
  2. Place the object you want to scan in the center of the view with good lighting
  3. Click "Scan Object" - the scanner will capture multiple angles
  4. Slowly move your camera around the object for best results
  5. Wait for the 3D model to be generated on the right side
  6. Use "Export Model" to download the 3D model (PLY format)
  7. Use "Reset" to clear the current scan and start over

Note: This scanner creates a point cloud based on feature detection from your camera. For best results, use an object with good texture and lighting.
