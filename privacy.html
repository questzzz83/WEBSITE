Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy explains how luispaiva.co.uk ("the site", "we", "I") collects, uses, and protects any information you provide when you use this website.

Information we collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Newsletter subscriptions: If you subscribe to The Friday Money Brief, your email address is stored by Beehiiv, our newsletter platform. See Beehiiv's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.
  • Analytics: The site may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which articles are most useful. No personally identifiable information is collected for analytics purposes.
  • Affiliate links: When you click an affiliate link, the third-party provider may set cookies to track referrals. We do not control or have access to this data.

How we use your information

  • To send you the Friday Money Brief newsletter (only if you subscribed)
  • To understand how the site is used so we can improve it
  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties
  • We do not send marketing emails beyond the newsletter you signed up for

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies. Affiliate links may cause third-party cookies to be set by partner providers — these are outside our control. You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, reply to any newsletter email or contact us directly.

Third-party links

This site contains links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We recommend reading the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Affiliate disclosure

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase or open an account, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we would genuinely consider using, and affiliate links are always disclosed within articles.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reply to any newsletter email.
